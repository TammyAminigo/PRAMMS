Maintenance Requests

Submit, track, and resolve maintenance issues seamlessly during your tenancy.

How Maintenance Works

Things break, it's a fact of life. What matters is how quickly and transparently issues get resolved. Propz provides a built-in maintenance request system that connects tenants and landlords around property issues, keeping everything documented and easy to follow.

For Tenants

  • Submit requests: describe the issue, set its priority (low, medium, high, or emergency), and submit it to your landlord.
  • Attach photos: upload images of the issue for clarity.
  • Track progress: see real-time status updates as your request moves through the resolution process.

For Landlords

  • Get notified: see new requests in your dashboard with a notification badge.
  • Update status: move requests through stages (pending, in progress, completed) so tenants always know what's happening.
  • Maintain records: all resolved and open issues are logged for future reference.
