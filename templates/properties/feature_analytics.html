Property Analytics

Clear insights for landlords: vacancy tracking, rent monitoring, and tenancy history.

Your Dashboard at a Glance

Managing rental properties involves keeping track of many moving parts. Propz gives landlords real-time insights into their portfolio, so every decision, from pricing to vacancies, is backed by actual data.

When you log in as a landlord, your dashboard provides a centralised overview of everything that matters.

What You Can Track

  • Total properties: see your entire portfolio at a glance with availability status.
  • Vacancy tracking: instantly know which properties are occupied and which are vacant.
  • Rent monitoring: track expected monthly income across all your properties.
  • Tenancy history: view past and current tenants for each property.
  • Application overview: monitor pending applications and respond quickly.
  • Maintenance summary: keep tabs on open, in-progress, and resolved maintenance requests.

Data-Driven Decisions

With all this information in one place, you can spot vacancy trends, stay on top of tenant applications, and ensure maintenance issues are resolved promptly, all from a single dashboard.
