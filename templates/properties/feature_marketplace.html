Browse Properties Publicly

The Propz marketplace is completely open to the public. Anyone can browse available properties, including houses, flats, apartments, commercial spaces, shortlets, and land, without needing to create an account or sign in.

This transparency is at the heart of Propz. We believe property discovery should be effortless. Whether you're casually exploring the market or actively looking for your next home, the marketplace is your starting point.

What You Can Do

  • Search by keyword: find properties by name, address, or description.
  • Filter by type: buy, rent, shortlet, or land, each with its own tab.
  • Filter by details: narrow results by property type, number of bedrooms, and price range.
  • View full details: see photos, descriptions, pricing, and location for every listing.
  • Land size converter: convert between square feet, square metres, acres, hectares, and plots.
  • Apply directly: when you find the right property, sign up and apply with one click.
