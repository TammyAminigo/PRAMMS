Transparent Connections

Apply for properties directly. Landlords review and confirm, no middlemen involved.

How Connections Work

Propz connects tenants directly with landlords through a structured application process. When you find a property you're interested in, you can apply for it right from the listing page. There are no agents or hidden fees, just a transparent process between two parties.

Landlords receive applications in their dashboard, where they can review tenant details and decide whether to approve or decline. Once approved, a formal tenancy is created, with everything documented and tracked within the system.

The Application Process

  • Step 1, Browse and Apply: find a property in the marketplace and click "Apply for This Property."
  • Step 2, Landlord Reviews: the landlord sees your application with your profile details in their dashboard.
  • Step 3, Approval or Decline: the landlord makes a decision, and you're notified of the outcome immediately.
  • Step 4, Tenancy Created: once approved, a formal tenancy record is created with agreed terms.
  • The system also prevents duplicate applications, so you won't accidentally apply twice for the same property.

Why Transparency Matters

Every interaction, including applications, approvals, and tenancy terms, is recorded in the system. Both landlords and tenants have full visibility into the process, reducing disputes and building trust.
