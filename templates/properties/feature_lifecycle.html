Lifecycle Management

From application to lease to termination, every step is tracked.

What is Lifecycle Management?

A tenancy isn't just about signing a lease. It's a journey with multiple stages, and Propz tracks the entire lifecycle of every tenancy so that both landlords and tenants always know where things stand.

From the moment a tenant applies for a property, through approval, active tenancy, and all the way to termination, every transition is recorded, timestamped, and visible to both parties.

Tenancy Stages

  • Application: the tenant applies for a property through the marketplace.
  • Review: the landlord reviews the application and makes a decision.
  • Active Tenancy: once approved, the tenancy is live with agreed rent, start date, and terms.
  • Termination Request: either party can initiate a termination request with a reason.
  • Mutual Termination: the other party confirms, and the tenancy ends formally.
  • History: completed tenancies remain on record for both parties' reference.

Built-In Protections

Terminations require mutual agreement, meaning neither party can unilaterally end a tenancy without the other's confirmation. This protects both landlords and tenants from unexpected disruptions and ensures a fair process.
